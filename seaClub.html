Tbilisi sea club

Tbilisi Sea Club is one of the most popular places in the city, and it is located on the shores of the Tbilisi Sea, on a small, beautiful cape. Here you can relax on the beach in the heart of the sun. There are arranged bungalows, water slides, children's playgrounds and pools. Tbilisi Sea Club also offers water skiing, sailing boats and boats for a walk on the sea, diving and parachuting. It is also possible to study sailing, water skiing and swimming.
